## Full description (4000 char max)

Oh the Places I've Been turns your travel history into a living map.

**Track where you've been**
Search any city and mark it as visited — add the date, a rating, notes,
and photos. Your map fills in as you go, with visited countries and (for
the US) individual states highlighted right on the map.

**See your stats**
How many countries have you been to? States? Continents? What percentage
of the world have you covered? Your profile tracks it all automatically.

**Follow your friends**
Follow other travelers to see their trips show up in your feed. Like and
comment on the places they've visited, and get notified when someone
starts following you.

**Free, with no catch**
No subscriptions, no paid tiers — just a free map of everywhere you've
been, supported by ads.
