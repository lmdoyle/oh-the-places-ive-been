Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Oh the Places I've Been ("the app") is a personal travel-tracking app where you mark places you've visited on a map and optionally share them with people who follow you. This page explains what information the app collects and how it's used.

Information we collect

  • Account information: your email address and display name, and a profile photo if you choose to sign in with Google.
  • Location data you enter: the places you mark as visited (city/state/country and coordinates), along with any dates, ratings, or notes you add. This is location data you deliberately type or search for, not your device's live GPS location.
  • Photos you upload: photos you attach to a visited place are stored with a third-party image host (Cloudinary).
  • Social activity: who you follow, who follows you, and any likes or comments you leave on other users' visited places.

How this information is used

Your visit data powers the app's core features: your personal map, travel stats, and — if your account is public or you approve a follower — the feed and profile that other users can see. We do not sell your personal information.

Who your information is shared with

  • Other users: if your account is public (the default), your visited places, ratings, notes, and photos are visible to anyone using the app. You can switch your account to private in Settings, which limits visibility to your approved followers.
  • Service providers: we use Google Firebase for account sign-in and data storage, and Cloudinary for photo hosting. These providers process data on our behalf and don't use it for their own purposes.
  • Advertising: the app shows ads via Google AdMob, which may use device or advertising identifiers to serve and measure ads. See Google's ad privacy policy for details on how Google handles this data.
  • Map and place search: place search is powered by OpenStreetMap's Nominatim service, and map tiles are loaded from OpenStreetMap. Search queries you type are sent to Nominatim to find matching places.

Your choices

  • You can make your account private at any time in Settings.
  • You can edit or delete any place you've marked as visited.
  • To delete your account and associated data, contact us using the email below.

Children's privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.
